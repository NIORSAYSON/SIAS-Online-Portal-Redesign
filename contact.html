FAQs

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

How do I log in to the CSPC online portal?

  1. Visit the SIAS CSPC Online Portal website and select your user type.
  2. Enter your Student ID and Password in the designated fields.
  3. Click the Login button.

Who is allowed to log in and use the CSPC SIAS online portal?

The CSPC SIAS Online Portal is exclusively for CSPC students, instructors, administrators, parents, and admission applicants to log in and use.

How do I reset my password if I forget it?

To reset your password if you forget it, simply contact the CSPC MICT Center at mict@cspc.edu.ph. They will assist you in the password reset process.

What should I do if I encounter technical issues or errors while using the portal?

If you encounter technical issues or errors while using the portal, please be patient and wait for a moment as there may be ongoing maintenance. If the issue persists, you can also contact the CSPC MICT Center at mict@cspc.edu.ph for further assistance.

Are only current students able to access the SIAS online portal, or can alumni and faculty also log in?

Unfortunately no. Only current students have access to the SIAS online portal. reason.

Is it possible to access learning classroom activities through the CSPC SIAS Online portal?

No, you cannot use the CSPC SIAS Online portal to access learning classroom activities. Instead, you should use CSPC LEONS, which is available at https://leons.cspc.edu.ph/.
